# Change log on 0.0.12

## Released comment & multi-comment  

- One-Line Comment  
```gblk
--hai halo hai
tulis("komentar berhasil!")
```  

- Multi-Line Comment  
```gblk
--[[
     hai halo hai
     :v
]]

tulis("multi line komentar berhasil!")
```
